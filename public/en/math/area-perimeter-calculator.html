Frequently asked questions about area and perimeter

What is the difference between area and perimeter?

The perimeter is the total length of the outline (metres, cm...). The area is the surface the shape occupies (square metres, cm²...).

What is the formula for the area of a circle?

Area = π × r². The perimeter (circumference) is 2 × π × r.

How is the area of a triangle calculated?

Area = (base × height) / 2. The height must be perpendicular to the base.

How is the area of a trapezoid calculated?

Area = ((major base + minor base) × height) / 2.
