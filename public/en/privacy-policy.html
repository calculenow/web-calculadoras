Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy sets out the terms under which Calculenow uses and protects the information provided by its users when using its website.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that you provide voluntarily when using our contact form (name and email address). Furthermore, for the operation of our calculators, no registration is required, and no personal data from the calculations performed is stored.

2. Use of Information

Our website uses the information to provide the best possible service, particularly to maintain a record of users if necessary to respond to their inquiries. Calculenow is highly committed to keeping your information secure.

3. Third-Party Advertising and Google AdSense

This website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements to our visitors. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this website. The use of advertising cookies enables Google and its partners to serve ads based on users' visits to this site or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

4. Third-Party Links

This website may contain links to other sites that may be of interest to you. Once you click on these links and leave our page, we no longer have control over the site to which you are redirected. Therefore, we are not responsible for the terms, privacy practices, or protection of your data on those other third-party sites.

5. Control of Your Personal Data (Data Subject Rights)

At any time, you may exercise your rights of access, rectification, erasure, or objection (ARCO rights) regarding your personal data submitted through our form by sending an email to contact@calculenow.com.

Calculenow will not sell, assign, or distribute the personal information collected without your consent, unless required by a judge with a court order.
