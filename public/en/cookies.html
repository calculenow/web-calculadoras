Cookie Policy

At Calculenow, we use our own and third-party cookies to ensure the website functions correctly, improve your browsing experience, and show personalized advertising based on your browsing habits.

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file that is stored in your browser when you visit almost any website. Its purpose is to allow the web to remember your visit when you return to that page (for example, to keep dark mode activated).

Types of cookies we use

  • Technical (Necessary): These are essential for the website to function. They allow, for example, the system to remember whether you have accepted cookies or your design preferences.
  • Analytical (Third-party): We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous statistics on the number of visits, the most used calculators, and session duration.
  • Advertising (Third-party): We use Google AdSense. These cookies allow for the most effective management of the advertising spaces on the website, tailoring ad content to the requested service or your use of our website.

Managing and disabling cookies

In addition to using our configuration panel (available in the footer), you can restrict, block, or delete cookies from this site or any other website using your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

More information

If you wish to have more control over the installation of cookies, you can install programs or add-ons to your browser known as "Do Not Track" tools, which will allow you to choose which cookies you wish to allow.
