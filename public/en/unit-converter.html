How to convert units correctly?

Unit conversion consists of multiplying or dividing by a conversion factor to obtain an equivalent value on a different scale.

Final Value = Initial Value × Conversion Factor

Questions about conversion

What is the decimal metric system?

It is the system of units based on the meter, where units increase or decrease in powers of ten.

Why doesn't the US use the metric system?

Due to historical tradition and the high industrial cost of changing all its infrastructure to the international system.
